Kathmandu

Kathmandu is the capital and largest city of Nepal. It is a vibrant and historic city...

Services Available:

  • Kathmandu Durbar Square
  • Swayambhunath Stupa
  • Pashupatinath Temple
  • Local Guides for tours.
  • Hotels and Guesthouses ranging from budget to luxury accommodations.
  • Trekking and tours to nearby destinations.

Pokhara

Pokhara is a picturesque city known for its stunning landscapes...

Services Available:

  • Boating and Paragliding on Phewa Lake and surrounding areas.
  • Hotels and Resorts offering panoramic views of the Annapurna Range.
  • Adventure activities including trekking, zip-lining, and mountain biking.

Chitwan

Chitwan is famous for its rich wildlife and natural beauty...

Services Available:

  • Wildlife safaris in Chitwan National Park to spot rhinos, tigers, and elephants.
  • Jungle resorts offering immersive jungle experiences.
  • Canoeing, bird watching, and elephant rides along the Rapti River.

Lumbini

Lumbini is the birthplace of Lord Buddha and a major pilgrimage site...

Services Available:

  • Pilgrimage tours to Maya Devi Temple and various monasteries.
  • Meditation retreats and teachings on Buddhist philosophy.
  • Accommodations ranging from guesthouses to retreat centers.

Bhaktapur

Bhaktapur is an ancient city famous for its rich cultural heritage...

Services Available:

  • Cultural tours of Bhaktapur Durbar Square and Nyatapola Temple.
  • Newari cuisine food tours and culinary experiences.
  • Authentic homestays with local families to experience Newari culture.

Nagarkot

Nagarkot is renowned for its breathtaking sunrise views over the Himalayas...

Services Available:

  • Hotels and resorts offering panoramic views of sunrise and sunset.
  • Hiking and mountain biking trails through scenic countryside.
  • Cultural experiences and village tours in the Nagarkot area.
